Understanding the shift

What is AI visibility?

AI visibility is how well your business appears when people ask tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, or Google AI for recommendations — instead of searching Google the traditional way.

When someone types "best plumber near me" into ChatGPT, it doesn't return a list of links. It gives a direct recommendation. If you're not in that recommendation, the customer goes elsewhere — and you never knew they were looking.

This is different from traditional SEO. A business can rank #1 on Google and still be completely invisible to AI. The signals AI uses are different — and most businesses haven't addressed them yet.

How AI chooses which businesses to recommend
📄
Structured, answer-first website content
AI reads your pages looking for clear answers to customer questions. Vague or promotional language gets skipped.
🏷️
Schema markup
Behind-the-scenes code that tells AI exactly what your business is, where you are, and what you offer.
⭐
Reviews, authority, and consistency
AI cross-references your Google Business Profile, reviews, and directory listings to verify trust signals before recommending you.
❓
FAQ content that matches real questions
AI answers are often built directly from FAQ pages. Businesses that have them — written the right way — get cited. Others don't.
